Add your Mapbox token

The basemap is drawn with Mapbox GL. To keep this viewer free to host, RadarNexus uses your own Mapbox public access token — it stays in this browser only (localStorage) and is never sent anywhere but Mapbox.

  1. Create a free account at mapbox.com.
  2. Copy your default public token from account → tokens (it starts with pk.).
  3. Paste it below. The free tier covers ~50k map loads/month.
